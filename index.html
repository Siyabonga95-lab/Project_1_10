Project Proposal: Cosmic Duel of Light and Shadow

1. Introduction

This proposal is for an anime project called Cosmic Duel of Light and Shadow. The idea is to create a short anime series (3 episodes for the pilot season) that mixes action, drama, and science fiction. The story focuses on two ancient cosmic powers — Light and Shadow — locked in a battle that could decide the fate of countless worlds. The aim of this project is to bring together creative students, amateur voice actors, and artists to produce something original that can be shared both locally and online.

2. Significance of the Project

Anime is a growing form of entertainment worldwide, but many young creators don't get the chance to showcase their work in a professional-looking format. This project will not only give us experience in animation production, but it will also give fans new content to enjoy. It can help build connections between students and the anime fan community, and maybe even open opportunities for bigger projects in the future.

3. Problem Statement

The main problem is that local creators often lack funding, equipment, and a proper platform to release their work. Even when the talent is there, without exposure or collaboration opportunities, ideas stay as just ideas. This project is a direct attempt to solve that problem by pooling resources, sharing skills, and actually completing an anime pilot that can be shown to the public.

4. Target Audience

  • Anime fans between the ages of 15 and 35
  • Students and young creators interested in animation, voice acting, or music production
  • Cosplay communities and fan art circles
  • People who enjoy original stories with a mix of sci-fi and fantasy
  • Anime companies and studios who want to discover new talent

5. Budgeting

Estimated budget for the pilot season:

  • Animation Production: R 50,000
  • Voice Acting and Sound Effects: R 15,000
  • Music Production: R 8,000
  • Website and Promotion: R 7,000
  • Printing Posters and Merchandise: R 3,000
  • Total: R 83,000

6. Showcase Times

As part of this project, anime short films, art, and story ideas will be showcased weekly. Here is the student schedule for online and small local showings:

Sunday 15:00 - 18:00 Special Showcase (student submissions)

7. Summary

Cosmic Duel of Light and Shadow is more than just a fun idea — it's a practical opportunity to learn, create, and share. By working together as a student project, we can produce an anime pilot that not only tells a great story but also shows what new creators are capable of. If successful, this could grow into a full series and become a platform for even more student and small creator talent to shine.
