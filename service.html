Our Services

As a student with a strong passion for anime, art, and storytelling, I started this project not only as a creative outlet but also as a way to support both established anime studios and small businesses trying to make their mark.

Inspired by the depth and imagination of anime like Bleach, I want to provide creative contributions that show how students can stand alongside professionals in shaping the future of anime culture.

What I Offer

The Cosmic Duel Project is built to support anime companies, independent creators, and businesses with fresh and unique ideas. Here’s how I can bring value:

Concept & Character Design

From sketches to polished designs, I create original characters and settings that can be used in manga, anime pitches, or branding projects.

Script & Story Development

Writing scripts and world-building has always been my passion. I help craft plots, dialogue, and lore that can fit into anime projects or promotional content.

Music & Mood Concepts

I experiment with soundtracks and background music ideas that can enhance mood, tone, and atmosphere for anime-style projects or small-scale animations.

Student Collaboration Platform

Even though I work alone, this project is a bridge. I invite other students and young creators to share ideas that companies might adapt, opening the door for new voices in the anime industry.

Who This Is For

My services are open to:

  • Anime Studios — looking for new concepts, designs, or fresh perspectives.
  • Small Businesses — such as manga shops, merchandise stores, or indie creators who want anime-inspired branding.
  • Student & Fan Communities — groups that want to create collaborative projects, fan events, or even small animations.

Why Work With Me?

I may be a student, but that is my strength: I bring originality, dedication, and a perspective unshaped by industry limits. I believe anime is for everyone, and my project exists to prove that creativity can come from anywhere — even a single dorm room.

By working with me, companies and creators not only get unique ideas but also contribute to building a bridge between professionals and student creators.
