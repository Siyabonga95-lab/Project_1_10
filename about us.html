About Us

I am a college student who has a passion for anime, art, and storytelling. I bring something different to the table — from animation and design to writing scripts and composing music.

My main goal is to prove that with hard work and dedication, students can produce something on the same level as professional creators. I also hope this project will inspire others to start their own creative journeys and collaborate across skills.

I got my inspiration from Bleach, an anime that showed me how powerful storytelling and imagination can be. This project is for all anime lovers who want to see more creativity come to life, especially here in South Africa where anime is still growing.

This is not a company team — just me, a student, trying to bring something positive to the anime world and to give others a chance to be heard.
